At The Open Window, we understand that plans can change. Since therapy sessions involve dedicated time slots reserved specifically for you, we request all clients to respect the following cancellation and refund guidelines to ensure fairness for both clients and therapists.

1. Cancellation & Rescheduling Policy

  • Cancellations or rescheduling requests must be made at least 24 hours before the scheduled session time.
  • If a session is cancelled or rescheduled within less than 24 hours of the appointment, the session fee will be forfeited.
  • Rescheduling requests are subject to therapist availability.
  • Rescheduled sessions are also governed by the same 24-hour policy.
  • Sessions purchased as part of a package are eligible for rescheduling only and are not eligible for refunds.


2. Therapist-Initiated Changes

  • Therapists reserve the right to reschedule or cancel sessions due to emergencies, illness, or unforeseen circumstances.
  • In such cases, clients will be offered either:
    • A rescheduled session, or
    • A full refund for the affected session.


3. No-Show & Late Arrival Policy

  • If you do not join the session within the first 15 minutes, the session will be marked as a no-show and considered forfeited.
  • No refunds or rescheduling will be provided for missed sessions or no-shows.
  • If you join late, the session will still end at the originally scheduled time to respect the therapist’s schedule and other appointments.
  • Clients are responsible for remembering their session timings. While reminders may be sent occasionally, they are not guaranteed.


4. Refund Policy

Refunds will not be issued for:

  • Late cancellations
  • Missed sessions
  • No-shows
  • Unused package sessions

Refunds are only applicable if:

  • The therapist cancels the session and rescheduling is not possible, or
  • A duplicate or incorrect payment has been made.

Approved refunds will typically be processed within 7–10 business days to the original payment method.

5. Exceptions

We understand that genuine emergencies may occur. Exceptions to this policy may be considered on a case-by-case basis at the sole discretion of The Open Window team. Approval of exceptions is not guaranteed.



6. Therapy Bundles

  • Therapy packages are non-refundable and non-transferable.
  • Package validity periods, if applicable, will be communicated at the time of purchase.
  • Missed sessions within a package will be counted as used if cancellation policies are not followed.


7. How to Cancel or Reschedule

To cancel or reschedule your appointment, please contact us via:

Email: hello@theopenwindow.co

Please include:

  • Your full name
  • Session date and time
  • Therapist name (if applicable)
  • Reason for cancellation or rescheduling


8. Agreement to Policy

By booking a session or purchasing a package through The Open Window, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to this Cancellation & Refund Policy.
