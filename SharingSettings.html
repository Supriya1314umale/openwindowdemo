Sharing Settings

Last Updated: May 20, 2026

The Open Window believes in giving users transparency and control over how their information is shared.

1. Communication Preferences

You can choose whether you would like to receive:

  • Email newsletters
  • Mental health resources
  • Workshop announcements
  • Promotional offers
  • Appointment reminders

You may opt out at any time through unsubscribe links or by contacting us.

2. Therapy & Wellness Information

Personal information shared during therapy sessions is kept confidential except in legally or ethically required situations.

We do not publicly share:

  • Therapy notes
  • Session details
  • Personal mental health information

without explicit consent.

3. Community & Group Sessions

For workshops or group sessions:

  • Participants are encouraged to respect each other’s privacy.
  • Users should avoid sharing personal stories or information from sessions outside the group.
  • The Open Window cannot guarantee confidentiality from other participants in community spaces.

4. Testimonials & Feedback

We may request permission to share:

  • Testimonials
  • Reviews
  • Feedback

We will only publish this content with consent and may anonymize information where appropriate.

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

We only share limited information with trusted service providers necessary for:

  • Scheduling
  • Payment processing
  • Website analytics
  • Communication tools

These providers are expected to maintain reasonable security and confidentiality standards.

6. Managing Your Preferences

To update your sharing or communication preferences, contact us at:

Email: hello@openwindow.co
