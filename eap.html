EAP

Support works best when it feels safe, credible, and easy to access.

  • Confidential support for employees
    Professional mental health care is available to employees and their immediate family members, with confidentiality at the core.
  • Comprehensive, clinically led services
    Our EAP includes short-term and long-term therapy, emotional wellbeing support, crisis intervention, and referrals when extended care is needed.
  • Accessible and human-centred care
    Sessions are simple to access, fully confidential, and delivered by qualified mental health practitioners.
  • Customised for each organisation
    EAP offerings are tailored to workforce size, industry context, and employee needs, ensuring support is practical, responsive, and clinically sound.
